Who We Are

TIU Impact Hub is a student-led organization at Tokyo International University, dedicated to fostering innovation, entrepreneurship, and leadership among business students. Our mission is to create a collaborative environment where students can develop their skills, connect with industry leaders, and make a meaningful impact in the world of business.
